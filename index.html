Measuring surface heat

Using raster imagery captured by the MODIS instrument aboard the Terra satellite, I captured a decade of raster data that recorded the average nighttime surface temperatures on a daily basis from April 1 to September 30 in the San Antonio metropolitan area and used it to visualize the urban heat island effect.
